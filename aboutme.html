About me

Hi! I'm Katarina, or Kaća, or simply Kat, Kate, Katie... the options are endless, really.
I come from a small town in Serbia where I graduated from High School, and after which I escaped to a bigger, yet another small town, becoming an IT student.
Throughout my life I loved anything related to art, drawing, creating random things... but with keen attention to detail, it's no wonder I found my passion in field of UI/UX design. Hence, next to being creative, I love to follow rules - I do everything according to the grid.
Other things that keep me happy are cycling, writing and reading, watching series and movies, talking with friends and meeting new people.
