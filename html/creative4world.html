Creative4World

Creative4World is an android app development company.

Company's idea

The company is established by 4 IT students in 2020. They provide service of app development for android operating system, with constant learning and improving their programming languages, thus creating more opportunities and options for the company.

Website

The company’s website is showing simplicity and elegance in color and format. Following the unwritten rule in design, logo is placed on the top-left corner, with company’s name next to it. On the top-right corner, the menu is shown with three tabs: About (about the company), Our team (introducing the four founders), and Contact page. About page gives a small intro, friendly-written for anyone to understand, with app samples of the company’s work. Our team page presents the small introductions of company’s members. Contact page gives a form that requires visitor’s e-mail address, subject of the message and the message itself in which the visitor can elaborate its requests or questions.
