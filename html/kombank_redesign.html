KomBank mBanking

“KomBank mBanking is a service of Komercijalna banka that enables you to perform all the financial transactions via your mobile device at any time and place in a fast, easy and safe way.” (Google Play)

Problem

  • Poor color combinations (“noisy” background image).
  • Tiny CTA, and rather unnoticeable at some places.
  • Too many elements make the app look “busy” and “crowded”.
  • Use of capital letters in narrowed lists creating difficulty reading and following information.
  • Lists’ overview is tiny and dense (no proper usage of whitespace between items and borders in the list).
  • Disorganized menu with endless scroll of the sub-categories (the user can have difficulty pinpointing what they need/want to select).
  • Inconsistent icons and improper placement.
  • Bad transaction between activities (always goes left).

Next to personal notices, I’ve also researched google play’s reviews and extracted some of other user’s issues with the app’s design. *About 90% of the read comments were negative, and excluding the app’s functionality, most common reviews said: “Not convenient”, “Bad optimization”, “Complicated and confusing interface”, “Unintuitive”, “Illogically organized app with bad interface”.
